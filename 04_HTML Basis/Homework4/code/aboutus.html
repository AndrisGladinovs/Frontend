Baltic States is a non-profit organization founded by the support of the European Foundation for Development and Solidarity. Our aim is to inform website visitors about the tourism and business possibilities in all three Baltic regions.

For example:

  • popular holidays destinations
  • requirements for obtaining a residence permit in the Baltic States
  • legal procedure for getting a work/study Visa permit
  • opening of a commercial activity and their taxation
Our offices are located in Riga, Tallinn and Vilnius. Due to the current situation with COVID-19 and governmental restrictions for personal contact, please send us all questions through the provided e-form or simply send your written request per post using one of our provided legal address.
